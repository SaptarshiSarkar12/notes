# Pie Chart

Pie charts are very simple and easily depicted using mermaid. Here's a simple example:

```mermaid
pie
  title A Pie Chart
  "Slice A" : 5
  "Slice B" : 35
```

```mermaid
pie

  "Slice A" : 5

```
